Baked Salmon Fillets Dijon

Learn how to bake salmon at 400 degrees Fahrenheit with this easy recipe for salmon fillets coated with Dijon-style mustard and seasoned bread crumbs drizzled with butter

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces of fillets salmon
  • 3 tablespoons of prepared Dijon-style mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • a quarter cup of Italian-style dry bread crumbs
  • a quarter cup of butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a shallow baking pan with aluminum foil.
  2. Place salmon filletsskin-side down on the prepared baking pan. Spread a thin layer of mustard on top of each fillet; season with salt and pepper. Top with bread crumbs, then drizzle with melted butter
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
